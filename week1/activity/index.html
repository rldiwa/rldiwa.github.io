Scrambled Eggs
Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.
Ingredients Method
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream
  1. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat
  2. Mix eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Add to pan when butter is melted
  4. Gently stir eggs every 20 seconds
  5. Remove from heat when still moist
